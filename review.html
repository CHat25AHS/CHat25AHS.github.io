Transition

Transitions are simple 1 step animations that can be activated by hovering

Animations

An animation is a more complex transition where you can control what happens at what point in the animation and how it is activated

MP4

An MP4 is one of the most common types of video files especially when coding, although it doesn't always get supported by all browsers

MP3

This is just the audio portion of an MP4 file, it is a very popular type of audio file

Video

Moving pictures with audio that can be found on the web

Audio

Sound, most commonly used in coding for music and informational audio clips

Filters
