What Job Positions are Similar to Graphic Design?

There are many fields that relate to graphic design, it just depends on what aspects you like more that would determine what you would find better for you. If you like the more hands on approach you may like positions in wood working or metal working the most. If you like the more artistic side of things you may lean towards positions in painting, photography, or animation. Ultimatley it depends on your personal preferences.

Other Positions:

  • Interior Designer
  • Art Director
  • Web Director

There are many similar career options to choose from that relate to graphic design in some way, that means there are many different tools for many different jobs
