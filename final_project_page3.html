What Does the Job Entail?

Graphic Design can differ depending on where you work, if you work at fast food you could be doing anything from desiging cups or tray liners to making full advertising campaigns about a new limited time item. For store or manufacturing companies you could be making labels or graphics for containters and advertising new products. It depends on where you work but you will really be designing anything that needs to be designed online.

Different position can require you to use different tools, make sure you find a comfortable set up to suite your needs!
