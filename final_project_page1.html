What Different Types of Media do Graphic Designers Produce?

Graphic Designers produce a lot of different types of media such as posters, fliers, and even sometimes moving graphics. If you are looking at the advertising side of things your are more likely to be making posters or banners for ads. If you are making something more to inform people about an event you will be working with fliers where it is important to fit the most information about the place and location without adding too much clutter.

All of the advertisements are examples of what you might make or work with when designing.
