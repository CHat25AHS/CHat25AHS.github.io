What Role Roes Graphic Design Take in Advertising?

Graphic Design is seen everywhere in advertising, it takes on the role of being visually enticing to the audience. The goal is to draw the viewer in with cool graphics and then to hit on the information about the product or service being advertised whether it be through photos or text. There are some important things to keep in mind however when making advertisements.

Tips and Tricks

One important thing to remember when advertising is that if you are working for a client, they are the boss. You must work within their guidlines to ensure that they are happy with the outcome. Another important thing to remember is to never over design, the focus should be on the product or on the message. It is easy to have to many graphics that may overwhelm the viewer, you only need enough to grab their attention and keep them listening.
